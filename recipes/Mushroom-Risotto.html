Mushroom Risotto

Description

"The tantalizing aroma of mushrooms and butter beckons you to the table." - In-Game Description

Ingredients

  • Hylian Shroom
  • Hylian Rice
  • Goat Butter
  • Rock Salt

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients on a roaring hot cooking pot
  2. Wait until it's cooked.
  3. Eat!
