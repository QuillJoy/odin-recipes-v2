Egg Tart

Description

"You'll know this simple dessert is done baking when it smells just delightful." - In-Game Description

Ingredients

  • Bird Egg
  • Tabantha Wheat
  • Cane Sugar
  • Goat Butter

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients onto a roaring hot cooking pot.
  2. Wait till it's cooked.
  3. Eat!
